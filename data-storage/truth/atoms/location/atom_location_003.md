In software engineering job postings, "onsite" very often means "live nearby" — not literally in-office every day. There is approximately a 90% probability that a company listing "onsite" will agree to remote or hybrid once they confirm the candidate is local. This should be factored into location scoring: onsite roles in local cities should be treated as likely-hybrid, not strictly onsite.
