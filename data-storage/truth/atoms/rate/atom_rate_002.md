Full-time employed roles include benefits (401k, health insurance, and other benefits) worth approximately 30% on top of base salary. When comparing a full-time salary to a contract hourly rate, multiply the hourly equivalent by 1.30 to get total compensation equivalent. Example: $200k ÷ 2000hrs = $100/hr base × 1.30 = $130/hr total comp.
