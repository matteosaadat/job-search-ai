## Summary

BETA Technologies is building electric vertical aircraft and charging infrastructure. This role sits on their Digital Operations team — the internal AI-enablement group transforming the organization into a "fully AI-enabled enterprise." It's not a generic full-stack role; it's specifically about bridging AI models and production-ready user experiences.

The team is solving hard AI-in-production problems: memory, agent-to-agent collaboration, non-deterministic repeatability. The framing is systems-thinking, not prompt engineering. The tone signals a fast, autonomous, ownership-heavy culture — "ship your first PR on day one."

Compensation is $175k–$225k salary plus RSUs and PSUs. Full-time permanent. South Burlington, VT (on-site implied). Note: this is Mahdi's current contract employer.

- Experience on user-facing products with direct customer engagement
- Ability to work fast, adapt rapidly, move through ambiguity

- Contribute to architectural decisions for digital products
